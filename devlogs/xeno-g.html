Devlog: Xeno-G

Overview

As an alien spy on a space station, use your gravity manipulation powers to outsmart AI traps and navigate obstacles, aiming to reach the library for vital information.

Role

UI/UX Engineer and Gameplay Developer

Processes, Reflections, & References

  • Onboarding System: Created a tutorial system to introduce players to the game mechanics and controls.


  • UI/UX Design: Designed the game's UI, including the main menu, sections transitions and in-game HUD.


  • Gameplay Mechanics: Implemented Trap Platforms for the player. The ship houses deceptive platforms that, upon contact, propel the alien in the opposite direction of gravity. These traps, often accompanied by spikes, are cunningly placed to catch the aliens off-guard.
